Policy Alignment

NEP 2020 and NCF 2023 call for education that nurtures creativity, innovation, and problem-solving — moving beyond rote learning toward project-based, experiential discovery. IdeaSpark operationalises this vision by helping every student become a creator and innovator. The app develops competencies assessed by PARAKH and promoted by NCERT's innovation and entrepreneurship frameworks.
